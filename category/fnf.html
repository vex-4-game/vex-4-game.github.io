FNF Games

In this game, players take on the role of Boyfriend, a charismatic protagonist aiming to impress Girlfriend through intense rap battles against unusual adversaries, like Girlfriend's protective dad. FNF isn't just about hitting buttons; it's about sharpening your reflexes and syncing up with infectious beats to conquer each musical showdown.
